Resources for Cat Asthma

Welcome to our resources page, your comprehensive guide to understanding and managing asthma in cats. Here, we have curated a selection of journal articles, trusted websites, and other valuable information to help you stay informed about feline asthma and treatment options. Our goal is to provide you with reliable, evidence-based resources to support you in caring for your asthmatic feline friend.

Journal Articles

Journal of Feline Medicine and Surgery: Feline Asthma - Diagnosis and Management

Journal of Feline Medicine and Surgery.

This article provides an in-depth look at the diagnostic criteria for feline asthma, treatment protocols, and long-term management strategies.
Keywords: Diagnosis, Management, Treatment Protocols

JVeterinary Clinics of North America: Feline Respiratory Disease - Update on Feline Asthma

JVeterinary Clinics of North America.

A comprehensive review of the latest developments in feline asthma research, including new treatment options and management techniques.
Keywords: Feline Respiratory Disease, Asthma Research, Treatment Options

Journal of Veterinary Internal Medicine: Long-Term Prognosis of Cats with Feline Asthma

Journal of Veterinary Internal Medicine.

This study examines the long-term outcomes for cats diagnosed with asthma and provides insights into prognosis and life expectancy.
Keywords: Prognosis, Long-Term Outcomes, Life Expectancy
